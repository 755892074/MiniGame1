## 风格锚点（必须加到每张图的 Prompt 或 System Prompt）

**Positive 锚点（英文）**:
```
Japanese kawaii illustration style, warm muted pastel palette, low saturation,
soft brown outlines (#4A3B2E not black), subtle paper texture overlay,
hand-drawn imperfect lines, cozy warm atmosphere, rounded shapes,
gentle lighting with no harsh shadows
```

**Negative 锚点（英文，加到 Negative Prompt）**:
```
3D render, realistic, photograph, gradient, strong shadow, blur, text, watermark,
signature, messy lines, dark colors, scary, high saturation, neon, metallic,
sharp angles, thick black outlines, cel shading, anime style, barbie style
```

**关键风格规则**:
- 描边用深棕色 #4A3B2E，**不用纯黑**
- 配色低饱和度的暖色调（珊瑚/柔粉/薄荷/奶油黄）
- 背景叠加微妙的纸质纹理（paper grain texture）
- 线条有手绘的轻微不完美感（hand-drawn feel）
- 大圆角、圆润造型，避免尖角
- 光影柔和，没有强烈阴影
