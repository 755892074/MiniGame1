## 无厘头大挑战 - 插画提示词

### 题目插画

```
A funny exaggerated cartoon illustration in Chinese internet meme style,
[描述],
bold black outlines, simple flat colors, funny facial expressions,
512x512 square, no text, no watermark.

示例：
- "a chicken wearing glasses and looking confused while staring at a swimming pool, even though chickens can't swim"
- "a bar of soap that looks dirtier than before, with a defeated expression, sitting in a puddle of mud"
- "a soccer ball sitting in front of an open goal, but the goal has a tiny door that says 'no entry'"
- "a fish climbing a tree with a determined expression while other fish look confused underwater"
```

### 角色形象
```
A cute and silly cartoon character, a round blob with big expressive eyes,
wearing a tiny graduation cap tilted sideways,
arms raised in confusion/shrug gesture,
bold outlines, bright colors, clean vector style,
512x512, transparent background, no text.
```

### UI 装饰元素
```
Cartoon game UI elements in a fun colorful style:
[A shiny gold star with sparkles]
[A red heart with a happy face]
[A lightning bolt icon glowing with energy]
Flat vector design, bold outlines, vibrant colors,
256x256, transparent background.
```

## 通用风格设置

在 Image2 中建议的通用参数：
- 风格：Flat Vector / Cartoon / Illustration
- 比例：按规格设置
- 质量：High Quality
- 如果在同一章节，保持风格一致性
